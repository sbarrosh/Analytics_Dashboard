# 📊 Operational Workforce Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Operational Workforce Dashboard** designed to monitor and analyze team performance through both **Business Intelligence (BI)** and **Data Analytics** approaches.

The solution integrates workforce data and simulated operational metrics to provide visibility into key performance indicators such as attrition, attendance, absenteeism, and headcount. It is designed to support data-driven decision-making and identify operational risks.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Monitor workforce performance through key operational metrics
* Identify high-risk areas based on attrition and attendance patterns
* Provide actionable insights for decision-making
* Simulate a real-world operational analytics environment

---

## 🧠 Approach

### 🔹 Business Intelligence (BI)

Focus on **monitoring and reporting**:

* Development of interactive dashboards in Power BI
* KPI tracking (Headcount, Attrition Rate, Attendance Rate)
* Data modeling and visualization
* Real-time performance monitoring

### 🔹 Data Analytics

Focus on **analysis and insights**:

* Identification of patterns and trends in workforce behavior
* Detection of high-risk departments (attrition & low attendance)
* Comparative analysis across teams
* Trend analysis over time

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* **Headcount** → Total number of employees
* **Attrition Rate** → Percentage of employees leaving
* **Attendance Rate** → Percentage of presence across time
* **Absenteeism Rate** → Operational absence indicator
* **Operational Risk (derived)** → Combined impact of attrition and absenteeism

---

## 🗂️ Data Model

The project integrates multiple datasets:

* **Employee dataset** → workforce attributes
* **Attendance dataset (simulated)** → daily presence tracking
* **Derived metrics** → calculated KPIs using DAX

The data model follows a relational structure connecting employees with operational activity over time.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* KPI overview (Headcount, Attrition, Attendance)
* Attrition distribution and departmental analysis
* Daily attendance trend visualization
* Absenteeism analysis by department
* Automated insights highlighting high-risk areas
* Interactive filtering (e.g., by department)

---

## ⚙️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** (data modeling & visualization)
* **DAX** (calculated measures & KPIs)
* **Excel** (data preparation & simulation)
* **SQL / Python (conceptual usage)**

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Certain departments show higher attrition rates, indicating potential retention issues
* Attendance trends fluctuate over time, impacting operational capacity
* Departments with low attendance present higher operational risk
* Combining attrition and absenteeism provides a clearer view of workforce performance

---

## 🚀 Business Value

This dashboard demonstrates how data can be leveraged to:

* Improve operational visibility
* Support workforce planning
* Identify performance gaps
* Enable proactive decision-making
